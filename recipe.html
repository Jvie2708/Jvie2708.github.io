Sausage Bread

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 1 loaf serves 24 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour, Yeast, Sausage, Mozzarella Cheese(circles and strips),
Butter, Minced Garlic, (OPTIONAL) Green Onions, Water 		F=3 cups, Y=1 Tbsp, S=2 packages, M=8 circles and 4 handfulls of cheese strips,B=1 stick, G=2 tsp, (OPTIONAL)GrO=3/4 cup, W=Warm 1 1/2 cup

Step 1: Use flour, yeast and water to make the dough. Let it rise overnight
Step 2:Roll out risen dough. After that, get a small pot and use the stick of butter and minced garlic and melt it until the butter is a liquid the spread across dough.
Step 3:Cook the 2 packages of sausage in Medium High heat until sausage is cooked through
Step 4:Add the 8 mozzarella circles in a rectangle shape ontop of the dough in the center, add the sausage ontop and evenly spread it out, then get 4 handfulls of mozzarella strips to cover the sausage
Step 5:Roll dough into a burrito shape and place it on a cookie sheet with aluminum foil and non-stick spray and cook until the dough is cooked and the cheese is melted
Step 6(OPTIONAL):Add green chilies when adding filling

Freaky Fresh Taco

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Taco Shell, Pico, Refried Beans, Lettuce, Beef TS=1, P=2 tsp, RB=1/2 can, B=1/4 cup, L=1/4 cup

Step 1:Cook beef on Medium High heat until meat is cooked through
Step 2:Use the 1/2 cup of beans and cook in a separate pot for 12 minutes
Step 3:Chop up 1/4 cup lettuce on a small cutting board
Step 4:Get out pico and grab the taco shell and begin to fill
Step 5:Fill taco shell w/ beef, refried beans, lettuce and pico

BBQ Chicken

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken, Salt, Pepper, BBQ Sause Ch=1, S=3/4 tsp, P=3/4 tsp, BBQ=1/2 cup

Step 1:Thaw out chicken in sink with warm water.
Step 2:On a cutting board, season with salt and pepper
Step 3:Bring grill to 425 degrees
Step 4:Let chicken cook there until bottom side is tender
Step 5:Let the other side get the same way, check temperature with meat thermomiter and if it reads 165 it≈õ good to go
